Why I am interested in Programming

I found out that there’s a new field that I had to explore during my senior year. That’s called Computer Science. Growing up in the realm of natural sciences, I never had a thought of giving much attention to the growing impact of technology in all fields until I chose to challenge myself to learn how I could also use technology to advance medicine.

I enrolled into free classes on youtube to learn everything about frontend development and machine learning. Before I knew it, I was participating in the A2SV AI for Impact Hackathon with college graduates and students from all over Africa– a bold decision I took fueled by Thomas Edison’s story of declaring that he will light up New York City with Electricity.

Through learning, relearning, and unlearning a lot of things about this new endeavor, I’ve developed this personal portfolio and other projects like clones for other websites and models. I am currently working on a model that would facilitate Parkinson’s Disease patients. This model will give them access to specialized exercises programs and aid in keeping track of their medications.
